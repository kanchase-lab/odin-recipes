Classic Peach Cobbler Recipe

Description

Indulge in the sweet and comforting flavors of a Classic Peach Cobbler. This timeless dessert brings together juicy, ripe peaches with a buttery, golden-brown crust that is irresistibly crisp on the outside and soft and tender on the inside. Enhanced with a hint of cinnamon and nutmeg, this peach cobbler offers a delightful balance of sweet and spice in every bite. Perfect for summer gatherings or a cozy evening treat, this recipe is simple to prepare and sure to impress. Serve it warm, topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for the ultimate dessert experience.

Ingredients

    For the Filling

  • 6-8 ripe peaches, peeled, pitted, and sliced (about 6 cups)
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

    For the Topping

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup milk (whole or 2%)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the Peaches: In a large bowl, combine sliced peaches, granulated sugar, light brown sugar, ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, vanilla extract, lemon juice, and cornstarch. Mix well and set aside.
  3. Make the Topping: In another bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add the melted butter, milk, and vanilla extract, and stir until combined.
  4. Assemble the Cobbler: Pour the peach mixture into a greased baking dish. Spread the batter evenly over the peaches.
  5. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the peach filling is bubbly.
  6. Cool and Serve: Let the cobbler cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy warm, optionally with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.

Enjoy your delicious peach cobbler!
